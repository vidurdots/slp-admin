We provide a platform for community organisations and indivduals to raise funds through the use of everyday internet service’s.

Therefore if your an organisation with 100 members and all them were to use Bill swapping service you could earn £10000 worth of funding for your organisation.

This is just one example of a revenue models on the internet that can be re purposed for the good of the community.
